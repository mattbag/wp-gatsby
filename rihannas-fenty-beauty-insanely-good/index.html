Rihanna’s Fenty Beauty Is Here At Last And It Is PERFECTION

FENTY Beauty is here and guess what – it is EVERYTHING you wished for.

We were lucky enough to get a sneak peak before the range was released to the public and heck … we’re kind of speechless and frankly – that does not happen often.

The FENTY range, which you’ll already know is the brainchild of our favourite rock rebel Rihanna, is available at Sephora which is where we had the opportunity to get up close and personal with it early today.

There was a LOT of secrecy around this range. For real … For example, we had to sign a non-disclosure agreement before we were even allowed to see it. Sign it twice in fact.

What’s a non-disclosure agreement? Well, it’s a legally-binding document in which you swear to secrecy but guess what – we’re going to spill the beans anyway – well some of them. Because, like you, we have access to the internet and there is a LOT of information out there about this range and while we can’t tell you everything – we can certainly report on what other people are saying – and so far, it is ALL positive!

We can see why too. This range might be celebrity-endorsed but it has much more going for it than just that. Indeed, it really is quite something.

For starters, the packaging is cool as heck and much of it in a super-modern hexagonal shape.

The textures are creamy and lush and the colours flattering and seriously pretty. There’s creamy, natural foundations and shimmery, flattering highlighters. There are primers that actually work (yep – for real), skin sticks, lipsticks and tools too. We’re talking soft, fluffy brushes, blotting powders and much, much more.

Will you want some? Damn straight you will. The only question will be just how much. A little? Maybe? A lot? More than likely. Hop on over to Sephora and get yours now. And if we elbow you out of the way don’t take it personally okay? We just really want this stuff – bad.
